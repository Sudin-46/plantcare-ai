🌿 Nutrient Deficiency Guidance

🟡 Nitrogen Deficiency

Symptoms: Yellowing of older leaves, slow growth

Cause: Low nitrogen in soil

Solution: Apply urea, compost

🟤 Potassium Deficiency

Symptoms: Brown edges, leaf burn

Cause: Lack of potassium

Solution: Use potash fertilizer

🟣 Phosphorus Deficiency

Symptoms: Purple leaves, stunted growth

Cause: Poor nutrient uptake

Solution: Add bone meal

🟢 Magnesium Deficiency

Symptoms: Yellow between veins

Cause: Nutrient imbalance

Solution: Spray Epsom salt

⚪ Iron Deficiency

Symptoms: Yellow leaves with green veins

Cause: High soil pH

Solution: Apply iron spray

About PlantCare

PlantCare is an AI-powered plant health monitoring system designed to detect nutrient deficiencies in crops using advanced deep learning techniques and image processing.

The system uses a pretrained Convolutional Neural Network (MobileNetV2) to analyze leaf images and classify nutrient deficiencies such as Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium, Magnesium, and Iron.

In addition to image-based prediction, PlantCare incorporates a rule-based symptom analysis module where users can describe plant conditions. This hybrid approach improves the accuracy of diagnosis by combining visual and textual data.

The system provides actionable recommendations, helping farmers and gardeners quickly identify issues and apply appropriate treatments such as fertilizers or soil adjustments.

PlantCare aims to support smart agriculture by reducing manual effort, improving crop yield, and promoting sustainable farming practices through intelligent decision-making.

Key Features

  • 📸 Image-based nutrient deficiency detection
  • 🧠 Symptom-based text analysis
  • 🔗 Hybrid AI prediction system
  • 📊 Real-time results with confidence scores
  • 🌿 Guidance and treatment recommendations
